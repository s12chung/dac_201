Having a visual hierarchy allows readers to easily scan the website by picking out what information is important. Hierarchy groups information, so users can remember the information easier.

Use font, size, weight and spacing to emphasize or de-emphasize elements to show their relative importance to other text. When selecting these typographic attributes, just remember to consider how the text stands our relative to other text and the text content's importance to the user (and their situation). This is very important in the design.
