Coming from a Computer Science background, I found that many developers lack the design knowledge to make their site look awesome. Typography was one thing that seemed really foreign to me. So I made this website to share what I learned.

Whether it's for a redesign or building a site from scratch, here's four quick tips on how to make web typography look better. These tips will help guide you on your typographic design decisions. There's also a Summary page for a quick reference. I will warn you that you should understand Cascading Style Sheets (CSS) to implement of these tips. Hope this helps someone!
